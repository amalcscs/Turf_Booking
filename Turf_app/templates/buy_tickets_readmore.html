Benefits of Using AstroTurf Football is a grueling sport, and the gridiron takes a beating. Your field should stand up to the pressure as well as your players do. Football presents a unique set of challenges for turf manufacturers — we need to protect players’ lower ligaments during play, attenuate shock to reduce the chances of concussions, all while providing the speed and agility players need to perform. To deliver these benefits at the same time, AstroTurf incorporates distinct technological innovations.
